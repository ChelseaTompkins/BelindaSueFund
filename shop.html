Serena Belanger founded Nothing is Impossible in memory of her Nana who passed from Ovarian Cancer. Ten percent (10%) of proceeds from the sale of her Blue T-Shirts will be donated to the Belinda Sue Fund in support of Ovarian Cancer.

Angel of Grace Awareness Bracelets proudly supports the Belinda Sue Fund by donating $5.00 with every purchase of a bracelet. In order to make sure that the $5.00 donation occurs, you must select the 'Add Family Fundraiser's Name' option and type "The Belinda Sue Fund."
